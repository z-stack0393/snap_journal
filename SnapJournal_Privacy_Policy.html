二、数据收集与使用 / Data Collection and Use

1. 本地数据
本应用的所有日记内容、图片与导出文件均仅存储在您的本地设备中。开发者不会上传、存储或访问这些数据。
All journal entries, images, and exported files are stored locally on your device only. The Developer does not upload, store, or access your data on any server.

2. 相册访问权限
当您导出日记为图片保存至相册时，本应用会请求访问系统照片库的权限。该权限仅用于保存图片，不会收集或分析您的照片内容。
When you export your journal entries as images to your photo library, the App requests access to your Photos permission solely for saving purposes. The App does not collect, analyze, or share your photo data.

3. 位置信息
本应用不访问、不使用、不存储任何位置信息。
The App does not access, use, or store any location data.

4. 订阅与付款信息
本应用使用 Superwall SDK 管理订阅逻辑和订阅状态验证。Superwall 仅处理验证订阅状态所需的必要信息（如交易标识符），不访问您的个人身份数据。
所有付款均通过 Apple App Store 处理，开发者不会直接接触或存储您的付款信息。
The App uses Superwall SDK to manage and verify subscription status. Superwall processes only minimal necessary data such as transaction identifiers and does not access personal data.
All payments are processed by the Apple App Store; the Developer does not access or store your payment information.

5. 崩溃与使用统计
本应用当前不使用任何第三方分析或崩溃报告 SDK（如 Firebase、AppCenter 等）。
The App does not currently use any third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs (e.g., Firebase, AppCenter).

三、数据安全 / Data Security

由于所有数据均存储在本地设备上，数据的安全性取决于您的设备安全设置（如密码、Face ID、系统加密等）。
开发者不会主动传输您的内容，也不会建立远程服务器存储。
All data is stored locally on your device. Data security therefore depends on your device’s own protection measures (e.g., password, Face ID, encryption).
The Developer does not transmit your content or maintain any remote server storage.

四、未成年人保护 / Protection of Minors

本应用面向 13 岁及以上的用户使用。若您未满 13 岁，请在父母或监护人同意与指导下使用本应用。
This App is intended for users aged 13 and above. If you are under 13, please use this App only with parental or guardian consent and supervision.

五、您的权利 / Your Rights

您拥有以下权利：
- 可以随时删除本应用及其数据（包括所有日记内容与图片）。
- 可以通过系统设置撤销相册访问权限。
- 可以通过联系邮箱要求删除或更正与订阅相关的数据记录（如 Superwall 账号绑定数据）。
You have the following rights:
- You may delete this App and all local data at any time.
- You may revoke Photos permission via system settings.
- You may contact us to request deletion or correction of any subscription-related records (e.g., Superwall account linkage).

六、隐私政策更新 / Updates to This Policy

开发者可能会不时更新本隐私政策。所有更新将在本应用内或开发者的相关页面中公布，并自发布之日起生效。
The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted within the App or on the Developer’s official page and become effective upon publication.
